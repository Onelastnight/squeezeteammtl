THE STORY OF
SQUEEZE TEAM MTL
MONTREAL UNDERGROUND AUTOMOTIVE CULTURE

Squeeze Team MTL officially started in March 2025.

It started from one person with a vision to create something cinematic, underground, and different for Montreal’s car culture.

The first Squeeze Team MTL meet happened in March 2025 and nobody expected it to go as crazy as it did.

Over 100 cars showed up. Fireworks were going off, cars were pulling in nonstop, and the whole atmosphere felt straight out of a movie.

One of the most unforgettable moments was a white Infiniti G37 sliding under the lights while people surrounded the spot filming everything.

From the start, media was everything. The edits, drone footage, reels, and clips were what made people want to experience the meets themselves.

The first posts from Squeeze Team MTL exploded across Instagram and TikTok and the community started growing immediately.

As the meets became bigger, police attention increased and events became harder to organize safely.

But the vision never disappeared.

The future of Squeeze Team MTL is about building something bigger, safer, more organized, and permanent.
